Biography

I am a second-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science department at University of Liverpool in UK, based off-site at Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University in China. I am fortunate to be co-advised by Prof. Kaizhu Huang, Prof. Rui Zhang, Prof. Qiufeng Wang, and Prof. Guangliang Cheng. I received my Bachelor’s degree in Electronic Information Science and Technology from Heilongjiang University and my Master’s degree in MRes Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems from University of Liverpool. Previously, I have worked or interned at DJI and Duke (Kunshan) University. My research interests include: 3D Vision & Robotics. Feel free to contact me via email for discussions or any potential opportunities.
